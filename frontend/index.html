How it works

Five steps, one perfect watchlist.

From your Spotify library to AI-curated movie recommendations in under a minute.

🎧

Spotify Data

We read your top tracks, artists, audio features, and listening habits — securely, read-only.

📝

Lyrics Analysis

Genius API fetches song lyrics. HuggingFace DistilRoBERTa detects emotions in every line.

🧠

Personality Cluster

K-Means clustering maps your combined audio + emotion features into one of six music personality archetypes.

🎬

Movie Matching

Sentence Transformer embeddings and cosine similarity rank 5,000+ TMDB movies by how well they fit your vibe.

✨

AI Explanations

Groq (Llama 3) writes a personalized, one-paragraph explanation for why each film matches your personality.

📚

Session History

Every analysis is saved locally. Browse past sessions and watch your taste evolve over time.
